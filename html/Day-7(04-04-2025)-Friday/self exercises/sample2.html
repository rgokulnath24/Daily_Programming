In "Spider-Man" (2002), shy, science-obsessed Peter Parker is bitten by a radioactive spider, gaining superhuman abilities, including web-slinging and enhanced strength. He struggles to control his newfound powers while also balancing high school and a crush on Mary Jane Watson. After a tragic loss, Peter embraces his destiny as Spider-Man, a costumed crime-fighter in New York City. He faces the Green Goblin, a powerful villain created by Peter's science teacher, Norman Osborn, who is driven by a desire for power and revenge. Spider-Man must learn to control his powers and make difficult choices to protect the city and those he cares about, ultimately defeating the Green Goblin and finding his place as a hero
